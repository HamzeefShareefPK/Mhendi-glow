### Tier 2: Guest Posting (Month 2–4)
Target these types of sites for guest posts:

**Bridal/Wedding Sites:**
- theknot.com — Submit "Bridal Mehndi Guide"
- brides.com — Submit "Top Mehndi Trends"
- weddingwire.com — Submit "Choosing a Mehndi Artist"

**South Asian Lifestyle Blogs:**
- Search Google: `site:.com "write for us" mehndi OR henna`
- Search Google: `"guest post" mehndi blog 2024`
- Search Google: `inurl:write-for-us beauty henna`

**Pakistani/Indian Fashion Blogs:**
- Look for blogs with DA 20–50 in the niche
- Offer a unique 1000+ word article in exchange for a dofollow backlink

### Tier 3: Pinterest (Ongoing — Most Important for This Niche)
Pinterest is the #1 traffic source for mehndi sites.

**Pinterest SEO Strategy:**
```
Board Names (keyword-rich):
├── Bridal Mehndi Designs 2024
├── Arabic Mehndi Designs
├── Eid Mehndi Ideas
├── Pakistani Mehndi Designs
├── Simple Mehndi for Beginners
├── Minimal Mehndi Designs
├── Finger Mehndi Ideas
└── Mehndi Tips & Tutorials

Pin Strategy:
- Post 10–15 pins per day (use Tailwind scheduler)
- Vertical images: 1000 x 1500px (2:3 ratio)
- Pin title: Use exact match keyword
- Pin description: 150–300 words, keyword-rich
- Add hashtags: #mehndi #henna #bridalmhendi #mehndidesi
- Link every pin back to your website
```
